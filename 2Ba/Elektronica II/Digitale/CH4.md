## 2 Laws and Rules of Boolean Algebra

### Laws of Boolean Algebra

#### Commutative Laws

* $ A + B = B + A $
* $ A * B = B * A $

#### Associative Laws

* $ A + (B + C) = (A + B) + C $
* $ A * (B * C) = (A * B) * C $

#### Distributive Law

* $ A * (B + C) = AB + AC $
